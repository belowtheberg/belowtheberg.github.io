Welcome to Below the Berg, a podcast hosted by two Purdue students striving to provide a platform for college-aged students that are elevating their college experiences via internships, startups, or simply doing their own thing. Through this platform we aspire to provide a fresh perspective from the eyes of the new generation of creative professionals across all fields. If we sound ignorant, we probably are. If we sound naïve, we probably are. This is the beauty of podcasting though, it gives college kids like us a chance to share the stories and strategies of the “successful” before they are labeled as such.
