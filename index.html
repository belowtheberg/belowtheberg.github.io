About

My name is Evan Pesut, I am an Environmental and Natural Resources Engineering Major at Purdue University. Below the Berg is a podcast with the vision to provide a platform for college trailblazers that are not simply waiting for their degree to make an impact beyond the tunnel vision of academic success. I am driven to establish an environment of ambitious positivity that is accessible to high school and early college students that are seeking exposure to conversations with college entrepreneurs, students with exceptional internship experience, and extracurricular leaders across all majors and industries.
